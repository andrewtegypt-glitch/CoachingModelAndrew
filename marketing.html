🎥 Content Creation Setup

What You Need to Film Reels

✅ Smartphone with good camera (iPhone 13+ or equivalent)
✅ Ring light or window light (natural light is free and great)
✅ Simple background (clean wall, bookshelf, or plain backdrop)
✅ Tripod or phone stand
✅ CapCut (free app) for subtitles and editing
✅ Canva (free) for carousels, quote cards, and story graphics

Canva Brand Settings

Primary Color: #c9a84c (Gold)
Secondary Color: #08111f (Dark Navy)
Text Color: #edf2ff (Off White)
Accent Color: #5b84ff (Blue)
Font: Inter or Montserrat (Bold for headlines, Regular for body)

Filming Tips

✅ Film in landscape first, crop to vertical for Reels
✅ Always use subtitles - 85% of Reels watched without sound
✅ Face the light source, never have it behind you
✅ Speak 20% slower than feels natural on camera
✅ Film 2-3 takes per reel, use the most natural-sounding one

Pro tip: Batch film 4-5 reels in one sitting. Same outfit, same location. It's efficient and you'll be warmed up by reel 3.

📱 WhatsApp Strategy

WhatsApp is Egypt's most personal digital channel. Use it for warm outreach and community building, not broadcasting.

Week 1: Warm Launch

Send a personalised message to your top 50 professional contacts. Not a broadcast - personal messages. Tell them you've launched, share your site, offer them a free session or ask them to think of someone who'd benefit.

Weekly Broadcast (existing contacts who opt in)

Sunday evening: 1 leadership tip or reflection. 3-4 sentences. No hard sell. Occasional reminder of free sessions remaining. Keep the broadcast list under 200 people - quality over volume.
