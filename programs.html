Free Discovery Call Free • 45 min

A no-pressure conversation to understand where you are, where you want to go, and whether coaching is the right next step.

What we cover

  • Current role context and key pressures
  • Top 1–2 goals or blockers you want to shift
  • Working style, values and non-negotiables
  • Fit-check: is coaching the right tool right now?
  • Recommended tier and next steps

You walk away with

  • A short written reflection on what I heard
  • One immediate experiment to try this week
  • A clear recommendation — even if it's not working with me

The Coaching App — Concept Mockup

A companion app that sits between sessions, acting as a bridge between your real life and your coaching work.

Key features

Daily check-in

2-minute energy, mood & focus pulse. Data builds patterns your coach can see.

Win tracker

Log small wins. The app reminds you what's going well when doubt creeps in.

Focus of the week

One action set by you and your coach. Gentle nudges, not nagging.

Session prep

Auto-generated brief from your week's data. No more blank-page starts.

ADHD mode

Shorter prompts, dopamine streaks, overwhelm check-ins, task capture.

Enterprise view

Aggregate dashboards for HR sponsors. Individual data stays private.
