Free Discovery (Launch tier)

Free — first 50 clients 45 min • No obligation

Who it's for

First-time coaching clients exploring fit. Used as a funnel into paid tiers.

Session structure

  • 0–10 min: Context — current role, pressures, 1 key challenge
  • 10–30 min: Mini-coaching on the challenge (one actionable reframe)
  • 30–40 min: Walk through program fit & tier recommendation
  • 40–45 min: Next step + calendar link

Supporting material

  • Pre-call intake form (5 Qs)
  • Post-call summary PDF with 1 recommended action
  • Tier comparison one-pager

Clarity Session (Entry tier)

$75 1 x 60 min + action plan

Who it's for

Mid-level professionals facing one specific challenge (promotion, team conflict, burnout). Quick unblock, no long commitment.

Session structure (60 min)

  • 0–10 min: Situational deep-dive & framing
  • 10–25 min: Root-cause exploration (5-whys or SCARF)
  • 25–45 min: Solution design — 3 options, pick one
  • 45–55 min: Implementation plan (who/what/when)
  • 55–60 min: Commitment & accountability hook

Content covered

  • Single-challenge diagnostic
  • Decision framework tailored to the issue
  • One 7-day micro-plan

Supporting material

  • Personalised action plan PDF (sent within 24h)
  • Optional 15-min follow-up voice note at day 7
  • Curated reading list (2–3 items)
