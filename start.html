Specialist Focus

ADHD-Aware Leadership Coaching

For leaders whose brains work differently — and who are done pretending otherwise

Many of the most visionary founders, creative directors, and senior leaders I've worked with have ADHD — diagnosed or suspected. The challenge isn't capability. It's that most leadership systems were designed for neurotypical brains.

My ADHD-aware coaching adapts frameworks to match how you actually think: in bursts, visually, non-linearly. We work with your energy cycles, not against them. We build systems that hold up even on your worst days.

🟢 Energy Mapping

Work with your natural rhythms instead of fighting them daily.

🟢 Systems That Stick

Structures built for high-variance energy and attention patterns.

🟢 Decision Clarity

Tools to cut through overwhelm and make faster, cleaner calls.

🟢 Team Communication

How to lead effectively when your style differs from the norm.

FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started
How is this different from other executive coaching?▼
Most coaching is generic. My work is rooted in the Egypt and MENA business context, built around your actual situation, and adapted for leaders who may think or work differently. I bring 15+ years of real leadership experience — not just theory.
Do I need to be diagnosed with ADHD to work with you?▼
No. Many of my clients suspect they may have ADHD but have never been formally diagnosed. Others are simply high-performers who feel their current systems aren't built for how they think. ADHD-aware coaching benefits anyone who works in bursts, struggles with traditional productivity systems, or needs a different approach.
Where do sessions take place?▼
Sessions are available in-person in Cairo or El Gouna, or via Zoom for remote clients across Egypt and the wider MENA region. All formats are equally effective.
How quickly can I get started?▼
Book a free discovery session and we can typically start within 1-2 weeks. Discovery sessions are available most weekdays and can be scheduled via Calendly.
What is the pricing structure?▼
Programs start from EGP 8,000 for the 4-week Clarity Sprint up to EGP 60,000+ for the 6-month Executive Partner retainer. Payment plans are available. The discovery session is always free.
Is coaching confidential?▼
Completely. Everything discussed in coaching sessions is 100% confidential. I work under a strict professional coaching ethics code.
